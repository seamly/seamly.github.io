Welcome to Seamly

Seamly supports fashion tech designs which use new materials and electronic circuitry. Changes made in Virtual Reality are connected to production patterns to provide the maximum creative experience, boost profits, and increasing scalability.

IRL or digital, you decide

Use Seamly to create clothing IRL for yourself, family, and friends. You can even create digital clothing for your 3D characters. Dress yourself and your character in the same design!
